Hi. I'm Laura.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in blogs and books, I can be found spending time with my family enjoying camping, hiking, and fishing.

I was born and raised in a small town in Alberta, Canada. My family always called me the wild child as I spent most of my days outside in nature and only came in when I got hungry or tired. A true country girl.

My passion: I love photography - it's one of the most powerful methods to capture a moment in time and relay a story with one image. Landscape photos are my favourite, with portrait photos coming in a close second.

A fun fact: As a country girl, growing up you might think I had experience with horses and cattle and such but I only learned to ride a horse at 18. Before then, it was dirt bikes or pedal bikes to go somewhere fast!
